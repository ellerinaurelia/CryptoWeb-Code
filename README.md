## 🌍 What on Earth is Cryptex?

Cryptex is a hyper-modern, buttery-smooth web interface designed for traders who value aesthetics as much as their portfolios. We built this because we believe that even if you're losing money in a bear market, the UI you're staring at should at least look beautiful. 💸✨

## 🧩 Anatomy of Cryptex (The 9 Pillars)

1. **`Preloader.tsx`**: Killed the ugly loading flash.
2. **`Header.tsx`**: Sleek navigation for the Cryptex spaceship.
3. **`Hero.tsx`**: The eye-catching landing section.
4. **`MarketUpdate.tsx`**: The Heart Attack Machine (Real-time trends).
5. **`CryptoTable.tsx`**: The Ledger of Truth (Sorted coin data).
6. **`Features.tsx`**: Why we're better than the competition.
7. **`CallToAction.tsx`**: The FOMO Injector.
8. **`Testimonials.tsx`**: The Hopium Den (Review cards).
9. **`Footer.tsx`**: The legal basement.
